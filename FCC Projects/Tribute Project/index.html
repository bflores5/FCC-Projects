Timeline of Bray Wyatt's life:

  • 1987 - The Rotunda family brings home Windham Rotunda from Brooksville, Florida.
  • 2005 - Attends Hernando High School and joins wrestling to win a state championship.
  • - Attends College of the Sequioas then Troy University to play football.
  • 2009 - Decides to pursue professional wreslting as a career and makes debut for FCW (Florida Championship Wreslting) where he also wins his first championship with his brother Taylor Rotunda.
  • 2010 - Joins NXT a WWE show alongside Husky Harris and Cody Rhodes.
  • 2011 - Returns to FCW to join his brother Taylor.
  • 2012 - Rotunda becomes Bray Wyatt in NXT from Eli Cottonwood in FCW and joins a stable named the Wyatt Family.
  • 2013 - Bray Wyatt joins the main roster in WWE Raw with his stable the Wyatt Family.
  • 2016 - Bray Wyatt wins his first WWE title (Smackdown Tag Team Championship).
  • 2017 - Bray Wyatt wins the WWE Championship for the first time.
  • 2018 - Tag Teams with Matt Hardy.
  • 2019 - Creates his new character "The Fiend" and wins the Universal Championship.
  • 2021 - He is released from WWE.
  • 2022 - He returns to WWE with his brother who potrays as Uncle Howdy and has his last televised match with LA Knight.
  • 2023 - Bray Wyatt's last appearance in WWE (February 17, 2023) to battle personal health issues that would eventually lead to his death.
